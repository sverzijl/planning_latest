  - Morning trucks depart at 8am Monday
  - They can ONLY load D-1 production (Sunday)
  - Physical constraint: Cannot load same-day production before 8am
  - Monday demand requires these morning trucks
  - Therefore: Sunday production is REQUIRED, not a modeling bug

because the alternative (missing Monday deliveries) has infinite cost.
